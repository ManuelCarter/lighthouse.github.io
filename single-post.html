Standing Against All odds

By Michael Smithson

5 Hours Ago

1281 Views

3 comments

Have you been told you don't fit in? Maybe something different like you don't belong here or you are incapable because of the physical energy around you. You need to know that you are the dictator of your life, people opinions or suggestions about where you really belong or what you fit into should not be mixed up with your passion.

Don’t expect life to love you always! In life, you shall sometimes see true rejection and know the real meaning of rejection but when your resolve to succeed is unshakable, no 'odds' can stand between you and your success.

You should die trying and fail than sitting and not trying to fail. Your passion comes with fears but choosing not to fall-in or give-in to them is the first step of success and what comes next after beating down this first stage is what you hear or see which has to do with the physical. This will try to break you but for you to overcome the first stage of negativity, you can step over the second stage.

The only things we are in control of are the choices we make at any given moment. Sometimes, things may seems weigh up than your burning passion but it is not. Take it as a game which you are so determined to get the highest score. Your courage and believe in what and who you are, should be firm like a rock and unshaken against all odds.

“Success is for those that presses on when they see a wall, find ways around a road block and consistently push boundaries and conquer territories against all odds.”

When the 'no' out-weighs the 'yes' faith become the only bridge to your answer and do not be sad that all odds are against you; be happy that your real purpose is for you. Let no one or no words take your ground, find the hope against all odds.
