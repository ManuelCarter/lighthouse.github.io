Who We Are

Light House is an online magazine on self-improvement/motivation and life hacks. Started in 2019, Light House is a go-to place when you need inspiration, motivation and practical advice in life.

It started with a vision and ambition of a boy,with a burning passion to fix and be involved in something. As a social addict, but with less or little knowledge about blogging, started with a laptop sacrificed by a friend, no early morning coffee but water, only when needed. Keeping to himself everything he was working on, though not so easy but with the unseenable supports and prayers he gets, it was a see through.
Actually, the idea of Light House came from comments of friends on personality and the name Light House is an inspiration of where the boy came from (A home with no electricity). Light House is not a solo body but working towards building a big family of Motivation speakers/writers, Inspirational bodies and other activist.

There is simply no master of everything. Therefore,Light House is opening the door to allow great writers and experts to share their stories or publish tips or advice.
Feel free to send us topics you'd like to see covered, questions you have, gripes etc. We'd love to hear from you.

"Live for what today got to offer not what yesterday has taken away."
